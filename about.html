The Fleeting Moment

Shunkan (瞬間), translating to "the instant" or "a moment," is the philosophy that drives my lens. I am dedicated to capturing the precise, often fleeting, human element hidden within the grand scale of fashion and the brutal honesty of the street.

My work is characterized by a dramatic use of shadow and contrast, pulling inspiration equally from cinematic noir and the stark realities of urban life. I believe photography is not just about recording light, but about revealing the darkness—the space where true emotion resides.

Based between Tokyo and London, my fashion commissions focus on narrative and emotion, while my street photography remains a personal pursuit of unvarnished truth.
